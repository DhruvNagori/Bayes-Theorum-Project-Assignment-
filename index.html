What is Bayes' Theorem?

Bayes' Theorem describes the probability of an event, based on prior knowledge of related conditions. It updates the probability as more evidence or information becomes available.

Formula: P(A|B) = [P(B|A) * P(A)] / [P(B|A) * P(A) + P(B|¬¨A) * P(¬¨A)]
